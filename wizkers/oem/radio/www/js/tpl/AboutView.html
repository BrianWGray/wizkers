About

Wizkers:Radio is a rig controller app for Amateur Radio equipment.

Version 0.3.1

  • New instrument: Telepost LP100A
  • Bugfixes and improvements on Sark 110

Version 0.2.6

  • Sark 110: Scalar + Smith Plot (polar) chart
  • Elecraft KX3, KX2, K3: accurate LCD fonts for VFOA and VFOB

Version 0.2.5

  • Fixed FT857 font issue
  • New K3 experimental support
